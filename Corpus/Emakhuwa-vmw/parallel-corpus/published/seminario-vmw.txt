Mulopwana ookukhurya
Mulopwana mmosa aahirowa okukhurya empa ya amay’awe.
Mulopwana owo khaareera okukhurya. 
Nihiku nimosa aahireiwa wiira ooniwe.
Yaahokuxiwa ekuwo yaakhuxeriwe mweeri, yeepokoholelaniwe ohiyu khuheliwa mmwaapuni ni maasi khuthomeiwa.
Vawaale mulopwana ole, veeralyaawe kikhunule ele yaathomeiwe, maasi ootheene yahimmwareela erutthu yootheene.Vammwareelalyaaya vale, erutthu awe yootheene yaahokela onukha. 
Mulophwana ole voonalyaawe onukha n´we, ole aahotthimakasa mpakha omuro; Ole aahotuphela mmaasini wiira arape masi etthu yoonukha ele khiyaamala. Veeraawe kisepeke onukha m´we exiko awe yaahisikuwa. Ti vo, mulopwana ookukhurya ewusu, ti vo vanunkhaawe.
